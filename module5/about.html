Main facts

Initially I came to Finland for studies. I stayed for work and now Finland is my home. In 2012 I graduated from Saimaa University of Applied Sciences with Restaurant and Hotel Management bachelor degree. I have been working in quite few places. I have tried myself as a hotel receptionist, bartender and seller.

Last year I realized I would like to try myself in IT industry since it is one of the most fast growing and huge industry. I have been working in customer service industry for more than 10 years and I think it is time for me to try work with machines. In January 2021 I got accepted into Haaga Helia UAS for the Business Information Technology program.

So far I enjoy studying HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I would love to become a professional Web developer. I intend to proceed Software path in my studies. Don't know whether I become successful in programming languages, but I will do my best!
